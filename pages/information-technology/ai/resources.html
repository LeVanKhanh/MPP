Prompt Engineering

Resources for learning how to design and refine prompts to effectively interact with large language models.

Prompt Engineering Guide

Prompt engineering is the practice of designing and refining prompts to effectively interact with large language models (LLMs). It helps developers and researchers understand the capabilities and limitations of these models while improving their performance on tasks such as reasoning, question answering, and content generation.

Beyond writing prompts, prompt engineering involves structuring instructions, guiding model behavior, and integrating LLMs with domain knowledge and external tools. This guide provides key concepts, techniques, and resources to help practitioners build reliable and effective AI-powered applications.

  • GitHub Repository: dair-ai/Prompt-Engineering-Guide
  • Website: promptingguide.ai
