Theories of Motivation

Understanding what drives human behavior and performance

Published: December 17, 2025

Content Theories

Content models of motivation focus on what people need in their lives.

  • Maslow's hierarchy of needs
  • Herzberg's two factor theory
  • McClelland's three needs theory
  • McGregor's theory X and Theory Y
  • Alderfer's ERG theory
  • Mayo's motivation theory

Process Theories

Process theories look at the psychological and behavioral processes that affect an individual's motivation.

  • Adam's equity theory
  • Vroom's expectancy theory
  • Taylor's motivation theory
  • Bandura's self-efficacy theory
  • Skinner's reinforcement theory
  • Locke's goal setting theory
