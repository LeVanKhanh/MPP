About Me

I am just an ordinary guy. I know I can not change the world — but I believe I can change myself.

Now, I want to build a little planet of my own — a place where I can store what I have learned, capture what I think, and reflect on who I am becoming. Bit by bit, I will grow through each idea, each thought, each experience I hold onto.

And maybe, one day, this little planet will reach a greater world. Maybe, from something ordinary, something extraordinary will begin.

My Mission

I record what I learn and come to understand — to light the path ahead and nourish the soul within.

My Vision

To create a personal space that grows with me — a place to reflect, to dream, and to leave behind something meaningful.

My Core Values

  • Integrity: I am not perfect — but I strive to be someone others can trust.
  • Courage: Never give up. I choose to face challenges head-on — to be a warrior, not a worrier.
  • Collaboration: I believe in the power of working together. Shared goals, open minds, and mutual respect create something greater than what we can build alone.
  • Self-reflection: I make time to pause, look inward, and understand my actions. Through reflection, I discover where I can grow — and how to make better decisions moving forward.
